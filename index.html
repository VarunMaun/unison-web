WORK PROFILE

Unison Design Studio specializes in architecture and interior design.

It was set up in January 2020 by Hemant J. Turakhia and Sumeer H. Turakhia.

The Studio aspires to offer design solutions which harmonize the functional and aesthetic requirements, while taking into cognizance prevailing social, cultural and climatic conditions.

It aspires to serve the residential, commercial, educational, healthcare and hospitality sector.
